## Planning Poker

Enterprise-grade agile estimation tool for modern teams.

### Real-time Voting
Vote simultaneously with your team members. See results update instantly as votes are cast.

### Custom Decks
Choose from Fibonacci, T-Shirt sizes, Powers of 2, or create your own custom estimation scales.

### Jira Integration
Import stories directly from Jira and sync estimation results back automatically.

### Session Management
- Create unlimited estimation sessions
- Invite team members with unique session codes
- Archive completed sessions for historical tracking
- Configure voting modes (anonymous, auto-reveal, show cards)

### Advanced Features
- **Timer System**: Set time limits for voting rounds
- **Spectator Mode**: Allow observers without voting rights
- **Anonymous Voting**: Hide votes until reveal
- **Vote Analytics**: Track consensus and voting patterns

## Team Management

### Multi-Organization Support
Manage multiple organizations and teams from a single account.

### Role-Based Access Control
- **Organization Admin**: Full control over organization settings
- **Team Lead**: Manage team members and sessions
- **Member**: Participate in sessions and collaborate

### Invitations
Send email invitations to team members. They can join using their preferred authentication method.

## Authentication & Security

### Single Sign-On (SSO)
Connect with:
- **Google** - Sign in with your Google account
- **Microsoft** - Use your Microsoft/Office 365 credentials
- **GitHub** - Authenticate with GitHub

### Connected Accounts
Link multiple SSO providers to a single Teamova account for flexible access.

### Security Features
- End-to-end encryption for sensitive data
- Session-based authentication
- Automatic session timeout
- Role-based permissions

## Real-time Collaboration

### WebSocket Technology
Instant updates without page refresh:
- Vote casting and reveals
- Participant joins/leaves
- Role changes
- Story selection

### Notifications
Stay informed with real-time toast notifications for important events.
