## Sign In Your Way

You can now sign in to Teamova using your preferred account:

- **Google Account** - Sign in with your Google workspace
- **Microsoft Account** - Use your Microsoft 365 credentials
- **GitHub Account** - Connect with your GitHub identity

### Connect Multiple Accounts

Link all your accounts in one place. Switch between Google, Microsoft, and GitHub sign-in methods seamlessly from your account settings.

### Set a Backup Password

For added flexibility, you can now set a password even if you signed up with Google, Microsoft, or GitHub. Perfect for when you need an alternative sign-in method.

## Improved Forms

We've made forms clearer and easier to use:

- Better visual indicators show which fields are required
- Cleaner form layouts across organization settings, team management, and Planning Poker
- Improved error messages to help you complete forms faster

## Planning Poker Enhancements

### Instant Notifications

Stay in sync with your team:
- See real-time notifications when roles change during a session

### Smoother Collaboration

<Update label="January 2026" tags={["New features"]}>

## Welcome to Teamova

Teamova is now live! Here's what you can do:

### Planning Poker

Run agile estimation sessions with your team in real-time:

**Create Sessions**
- Start a session with a unique code that anyone can join
- Choose from Fibonacci, T-Shirt sizes, Powers of 2, or create custom decks
- Configure voting rules (anonymous voting, auto-reveal, show cards)

**Collaborate in Real-Time**
- Vote simultaneously with your team
- See results instantly when votes are revealed
- Track consensus with visual distribution charts
- Set final estimates with smart suggestions

**Manage Stories**
- Add stories manually or import from Jira
- Include descriptions, priorities, and tags
- Mark baseline stories for reference
- Export results to CSV

**Team Management**
- Assign roles: Host, Participant, or Spectator
- Change roles during the session
- See who's voted and who's still deciding

### Jira Integration

Connect your Jira workspace:
- Import stories directly into your Planning Poker sessions
- Sync estimation results back to Jira automatically
- Filter by issue type, status, and assignee

### Custom Estimation Decks

Create decks that match your team's workflow:
- Build custom decks with your own values
- Use pre-built templates
- Share decks across sessions
- Duplicate and edit existing decks

### Additional Features

**Voting Timers** - Set time limits for voting rounds

**Anonymous Voting** - Hide votes until everyone has voted

**Spectator Mode** - Let stakeholders observe without voting

**Session Archive** - Keep history of past estimation sessions

**Dark Mode** - Work comfortably with automatic dark theme

**Bilingual** - Full support for English and French

</Update>
